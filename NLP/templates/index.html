What do these tasks mean?

  • Tokenization – Splits text into words (tokens).
  • POS Tagging – Labels each word with its part of speech (noun, verb, etc.).
  • NER – Identifies names of people, places, organizations, etc.
  • Sentiment Analysis – Determines if the text is positive, negative, or neutral.
  • Stemming – Cuts off word endings to get a root form (e.g., "running" → "run").
  • Lemmatization – Converts words to their dictionary base form using vocabulary (e.g., "better" → "good").
